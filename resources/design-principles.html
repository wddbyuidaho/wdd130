WDD 130

Design Principles

Activity Instructions

Estimated Time: 20 minutes

This activity will have you watch a short video and review a few infographics made by the design company Paper Leaf to learn some basic design principles. These principles have been around for a long time and are equally applicable to any type of design you might find yourself asked to do. As you visit websites, view posters, read magazines, etc over the next few days watch for examples of their use. If you watch for them you will start seeing examples EVERYWHERE.
