WDD 130

Personal Site Home page

Last week you should have gotten all of your content into HTML for your three pages. This week we will focus on finishing up the Home page.

Activity Directions

  1. Home Page Grid

    Open the wireframe for your homepage first. If you printed it out last week find that copy if it still exists. We need to build the Grid for your layout. From the wireframe determine how many columns and rows you will need. Then create those with CSS. Then start placing your elements into the grid cells.

  2. Navigation

    Once you have the elements on your page in approximately the right spot do the same for your navigation. Depending on your design you may need another Grid.

  3. Font and colors

    Add your fonts and colors from the Site-plan. Make sure that your text remains readable.

  4. Spacing and Alignment

    Add padding and margins where appropriate to give your layout the right amount of white-space.

    Adjust the alignment of elements as well at this time as needed.

  5. Publish

    Validate your html and CSS, fix any errors, then submit the URL to your site in ILearn.
